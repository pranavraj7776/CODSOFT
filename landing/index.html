    THOR

The Avengers is a fictional superhero team that appears in comic books published by Marvel Comics.
The Avengers are a group of superheroes who come together to protect the world from powerful threats.
THOR, on the other hand, is one of the founding members of the Avengers.
The character made his first appearance in "Tales of Suspense" #39 in 1963.
THOR is the alter ego of Chris Hemsworth, a wealthy industrialist and genius inventor,
who builds a powered suit of armor to become a technologically advanced superhero.
